Real Questions is an online platform that connects people with politicians.

Whether it's climate change, health care, or infrastructure, Real Questions allows you to ask politicians the questions that matter to you. By making use of social media and Australia's largest online politcal database, Real Questions leverages your political question ensuring it gets the recognition and support it deserves.

Getting started on the Real Questions platform couldn't be easier. Simply create an account and start engaging with other politically minded people straight away.
